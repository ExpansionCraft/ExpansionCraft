Inportant Commands

CREATE factionsx.create Create a faction. DISBAND factionsx.disband Disband your faction. PROMOTE factionsx.promote Promote a faction member. DEMOTE factionsx.demote Demote a faction member. POWER factionsx.power View your own power. ALLY factionsx.ally Ally a faction. ENEMY factionsx.enemy Enemy a faction. NEUTRAL factionsx.neutral Go back to neutral relation with a faction. CHAT factionsx.chat Use chat channels. IGNORE factionsx.ignore Ignore specific channels. NEAR factionsx.near View nearby faction members. LIST factionsx.list List factions. WARP factionsx.warp Use warp command. WHO factionsx.who Use who command. PREFIX factionsx.prefix Set the prefix of a player in faction. RENAME factionsx.rename Rename your faction. TRUCE factionsx.truce Truce a faction. HERE factionsx.here View current faction's land. KICK factionsx.kick Kick people from your faction. SHOW factionsx.show View your faction's stats. CREDITS factionsx.credits Bring up the credits menu. MAP factionsx.map View the faction map. HOME factionsx.home Go to your faction's home. SETHOME factionsx.sethome Set your faction's home. CLAIM factionsx.claim Claim land for faction. UNCLAIM factionsx.unclaim Unclaim land for faction. UNCLAIMALL factionsx.unclaimall Unclaim ALL your faction's land. AUTOCLAIM factionsx.autoclaim Toggle autoclaim mode. INVITES factionsx.invites View faction invites. INVITE factionsx.invite Invite a player. DEINVITE factionsx.deinvite Remove the invite for a player. JOIN factionsx.join Join a faction. LEAVE factionsx.leave Leave a faction. PAY factionsx.pay Pay tokens to any player. FLY factionsx.fly use the fly command. FLY_OWN factionsx.fly.own fly in your own land. FLY_WILDERNESS factionsx.fly.wilderness fly in wilderness. FLY_WARZONE factionsx.fly.warzone fly in warzone. FLY_SAFEZONE factionsx.fly.safezone fly in safezone. CHECK factionsx.check Check a players tokens. PERMS factionsx.perms Control your faction's permissions.
